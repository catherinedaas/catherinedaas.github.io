## About Me

Catherine is pursuing a master's degree in computer science through the CS@Mines Bridge program after transitioning from clinical practice as a physical therapist. Her current research through the DREAM scholarship focuses on machine learning, rehabilitation technology, and a balance-board-based robotic feedback system.

## About My Advisor

Dr. Ava Segal has a background in biomedical and mechanical engineering. She earned her doctorate in mechanical engineering at Mines and has been working on robotic rehabilitation technology. 

## About My Project

This project applies machine learning methods to sensor data collected from the Steadi Systems balance board. This will be part of data exploration from the NSF SBIR Phase I research study that is currently nearing completion.
