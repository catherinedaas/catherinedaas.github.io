Week 2 was 6/8–6/12. This week was still mostly focused on getting oriented. I reviewed key topics, including Cohen’s kappa for reliability, evaluating data for normality, and transforming data to better approximate a normal distribution when needed.

I also worked with the Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) through a computer vision exercise that generated a hybrid image of myself and my husband. If you are unfamiliar with hybrid images, the low-pass image, the woman with the dog, is visible from far away, while the high-pass image, the handsome man, is visible from close up. For reference, “far away” means truly far away. I had to walk about 8 feet from the computer to see it.
