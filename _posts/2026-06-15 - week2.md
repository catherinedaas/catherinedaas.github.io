Week 2 was 6/8–6/12. This week was still mostly focused on getting oriented. I reviewed key topics, including Cohen’s kappa for reliability, evaluating data for normality, and transforming data to better approximate a normal distribution when needed.

I also worked on Fast Fourier Transforms (FFT) through computer vision practice by generating a hybrid image of myself and my husband. If you are unfamiliar with these the low pass image (woman with dog) is visible from far away and the high pass (handsome man) is visible from close up.
